Recipes

Fish in Tomato sauce

1-3 serving

Whole Fish in Tomato Sauce is a delicious and conform hearty dish that features a whole fish pan fried and then simmered in a rich and flavorful tomato sauce. The combination of fresh fish with tangy, savory tomato sauce creates a satisfying meal. Perfect with bowl of steamed rice.

Korean Cucumber Salad

1-2 serving

Korean Cucumber Salad known as Oi Muchim is a simple dish to make and is often serve on the side along with rice and other dishes. It's a refreshing, slightly spicy that made with crisp cucumbers, dressed in a tangy, garlicky sauce.

Hearty Chicken Congee

1-4 serving

Hearty Chicken Congee is typically a porridge and is often eaten when you feel unwell or in cold weather. This dish contains thigh chicken pieces, ginger, lettuce, herbs, egg and preseved veg. Perfect adding chilli oil and fried dough stick - if no fried dough stick, can replace with yorkshire pudding!

Chinese Style Pork Curry

1-4 serving

Chinese Style Pork Curry is a combination with rice, potatoes, onions, pork and carrot. It is usually made with coconut milk to bring intense favour. This dish often served with salad on the side.

Bang-Bang Shredded Chicken

1-2 serving

Bang-Bang Shredded Chicken is typically a cold dish and usually served on the side with your main meal. This dish only take you 5 minutes to make. Ingredient includes mixture of soy sauce, sasame oil, vinegar, peanut butter and chill oil.

Korean Miso Spaghetti

1-2 serving

Korean Miso Spaghetti is a quick and easy dish to make on the go if you have a busy schedule! it is often mix with veg, choice of meat and miso sauce.

Garlic Butter Fried Rice

1-2 serving

Garlic Butter Fried Rice is s a flavorful and aromatic twist on traditional fried rice. It’s made by stir-frying cooked rice in a rich, buttery garlic sauce, often with additional ingredients like vegetables, protein, or herbs for added texture and flavor. The combination of garlic and butter infuses the rice with a savory, slightly creamy taste that makes it irresistibly delicious and comforting.

Chinese Style Shredded Beef

-2 serving

Shredded Beef is a flavorful and savory dish that features thinly sliced or shredded beef cooked in a rich, aromatic sauce. It's commonly found in Chinese cuisine, often served as a main course with steamed rice or noodles. The beef is typically tender, stir-fried with vegetables, and coated in a delicious sauce made from ingredients like soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and spices, offering a balance of sweet, savory, and umami flavors.

Hong Kong Baked Pork Chop Fried Rice

1-2 serving

This dish features a tender, crispy pork chop served on a bed of fluffy rice, typically accompanied by a savory sauce, and sometimes with vegetables or a side of pickles. It’s a hearty, satisfying meal that combines textures and flavors in a way that's deeply beloved in Hong Kong and throughout Southern China.

Kimchi Stew

1-2 serving

kimchi Soup (known as Kimchi Jjigae in Korean) is a beloved Korean comfort food that combines the bold, tangy flavors of fermented kimchi with a rich, savory broth. It’s a hearty, flavorful stew that’s often enjoyed as a warm, satisfying meal, especially during cold weather. The soup is known for its deep, umami-packed taste with a spicy kick, making it one of the most iconic and frequently enjoyed dishes in Korean cuisine.
